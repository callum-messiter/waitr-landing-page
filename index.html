waitr

Do you own a restaurant? Waitr lets your customers order and pay using their smartphones.

Demo

Pricing

  • No setup costs! You don't need any hardware; just a screen and an internet connection.

  • We take 7% of every order placed via waitr.

  • No win, no fee. If your customers don't use waitr to order, we won't charge you.

Benefits

  • Increase your Avg. Transaction Value. Customers spend more when they can place their order with just a few taps.

  • Reduce staff costs and free up your waiters to really look after your customers.

  • Turn tables faster. Customers don't need to wait for your waiters to take their orders or payment.

  • Personalise your outreach. Send emails to customers based on their previous orders.

Get Waitr

For Restaurants
  1. Create an account - for free!

  2. Upload your menu (or call us and we'll do it for you).

  3. Securely provide your bank account details (we use Stripe)

  4. Open the Kitchen page, and wait for orders to arrive.

  5. Easily accept or reject them; cook, prepare, and bring your happy customers their food!

For Diners
  1. Create an account - for free!

  2. Find your favourite restaurant.

  3. Browse the menu, place your order and pay.

  4. Sit back and relax. Keep an eye on your phone for real-time updates to your order's status.
