waitr

Do you own a restaurant? Waitr lets your customers order and pay using their smartphones.

About waitr

  • No setup costs! Sign up for a free trial over at our App for Restaurants.

  • We take 3% of every order placed via waitr.

  • No win, no fee. If your customers don't use waitr to order, we won't charge you.

How waitr works

For Restaurants
  1. Create an account - for free!

  2. Upload your menu via the interactive dashboard.

  3. Securely provide your bank account details (we use Stripe)

  4. Navigate to the Kitchen screen, and wait for orders to arrive on the screen.

  5. Easily accept or reject them.

  6. For the first 30 days, we won't take a fee. Afterwards, we'll take 3% of every order.

For Diners
  1. Create an account - for free!

  2. Find your favourite restaurant.

  3. Browse the menu, place your order and pay.

  4. Key an eye on your phone for real-time updates to your order's status.

Start Using waitr

  • For restaurants , we have an app for uploading your menu and managing orders as they are placed.

  • For diners , we have an app for ordering and paying at your favourite restaurants.
